### How do I fix a source error (403 Forbidden)?

<details>
  <summary>Add the relevant ```Image Proxy```.</summary>
  Add the following url to your image url request:
  ```
  http:localhost:3000/proxy-image?url=https://imgs-2.2xstorage.com/thumb/the-devil-butler.webp
  ```
</details>
